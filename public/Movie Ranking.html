Best Movies Of All Time According to Aryan

My top three movies are

A Dog's Journey

The story of a simple house dog through his several lifespans warms your heart , while his commitment to his bestfriend makes you cry on your knees.

Stranger Things

This is a series rather than a movie but the gripping story and charcter developments keep to you glued to your seat craving more.

Sardar Udham

A great depiction of how India and Indians suffered under British Rule.
